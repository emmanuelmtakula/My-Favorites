South Park

South Park is an animated tv show that is more offensive and uses comedy to offend and make fun of people. The main characters are 4 kids in grade 4 names Stan, Kyle, Eric, and Kenny. They live in a small town called South Park where something interesting is always happening every episode. Some key things about the show I like are:

  • The producers use humor to ridicule and make fun of people in real life.
  • The show expresses world issues in a humorous way.
  • The producers are not afraid to offend people with their jokes.
  • The 4 characters have diverse personality traits and have different ways of thinking.

Playboi Carti

Playboi Carti is a 27 year old artist from Atlanta, Georgia who started his career at the young age of 15. He made himself a name in the mainstream community when he dropped his self titled album in 2017. Ever since, he has continued to build a cult fanbase who enjoys all of his music. Playboi Carti is one of the most influental rappers of this generation for a few reasons:

  • He is very versatile and has switched up his style even if people didnt like it at first.
  • If he makes a song, you never know what to expect, keeps it fresh.
  • Playboi Carti has influenced many rappers, mainstream and underground and has positively changed rap.
